Future

My future plans with computing are to work in Data Science. I believe this has a good mix of applied programming and data analysis which I find to be an interesting mix. To get into this role I will most likely need a masters degree. After graduating, while working towards my masters, I plan to do either a software engineering or data analytics role. I also plan to get an internship in this field for relevant experience during my course.

One thing I'm not certain about is how AI will impact this job and other programming/data analysis roles, as this job seems to be something it can do effectively (See GitHub Copilot). I am concerned that AI will significantly decrease the opportunities in this field, so a managerial role could be an alternative.
